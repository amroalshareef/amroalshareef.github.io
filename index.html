About Me

I am Mechatronics Engineer graduating as a part of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) class of 2020 with a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering and Autonomous Machines. My research interests include bioinspired robotics and medical devices. I also like woodworking, sports, and making things for fun as hobbies.
