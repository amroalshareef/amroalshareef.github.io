6.146: Mobile Autonomous Systems Lab

Course Description

Autonomous robotics contest emphasizing technical AI, vision, mapping and navigation from a robot-mounted camera. Few restrictions are placed on materials, sensors, and/or actuators enabling teams to build robots very creatively. Teams should have members with varying engineering, programming and mechanical backgrounds. Culminates with a robot competition at the end of IAP.

Description

Our team for the MASLAB competetion consisted of 5 members: two of us had CS experience, three mechanical experience, and one electrical experience.
