Treehouse and Storage spaces

Treehouse

Description

After family friend in my neighborhood wanted a treehouse made for his children in his backyard, my friend and I took up the challenge. After a few weeks of planning and designing the 8 foot by 10 foot room with a porch and a 10 foot elevation, we began bying materials and constructing the tree house. After two months and a few mistakes and redesigns along the way, we finally came out with this finished product that has been standing for over two years now!

Storage Spaces

Description

A Middle School needed a way to increase the storage space they had for desks and chairs, so I built a loft within the storage closet they had that effectively doubled the space they could use.

Challenges

It needed to be structural enough to hold 700+ pounds and also allow for easy movement
