Treehouse and Storage spaces

Treehouse

Description

After a family friend in my neighborhood wanted a treehouse made in his backyard, my friend and I took up the challenge. After a few weeks of planning and designing the 8 ft by 10 ft room with a porch and a 10 ft elevation, we began buying materials and constructing the tree house. After two months and a few mistakes and redesigns along the way, we finally came out with this finished product that has been standing for over four years now!

Storage Spaces

Description

A Middle School needed a way to increase the storage space they had for desks and chairs, so I built a loft within the storage closet they had that effectively doubled the space they could use.

Challenges

It needed to be structural enough to support 700+ pounds and also allow for easy movement.
